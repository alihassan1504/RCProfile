Company Profile

Associated with the textile business for the last 50 years, which was started by our father as a trading house. Now as Ramadan Corporation (RC) we are a trusted name in the industry for its sustainable, ethical and compliant manufacturing of premium fabrics, apparels and sourcing. We began our journey with a mission to achieve excellence in fabric manufacturing including yarn dyed, chambray & lining, etc. and now provide supply and sourcing solutions, competitive pricing as well as customer service to our customers. Our team and founders are the reason for our success and the source of continued growth and expertise. We are very proud of our staff as they are young, passionate individuals with a strong team spirit. Our goal is to deliver a consistently high quality product, through our experienced team who will never compromise as our belief lies in “Quality is not an act it is a habit”
